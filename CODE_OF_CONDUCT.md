# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

CYPHES welcomes contributors who approach protocol design, security, and
product work with rigor and respect.

Positive behavior includes:

- discussing technical disagreements directly and constructively;
- separating verified behavior from assumptions and proposals;
- giving actionable review feedback;
- respecting different levels of experience and areas of expertise;
- protecting private information and responsibly reporting vulnerabilities.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, personal attacks, discrimination,
threats, deliberate disruption, publishing private information, or knowingly
misrepresenting simulated behavior as real network activity.

## Scope

This standard applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests,
community channels, and public representation of the project.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the CYPHES organization maintainers.
Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, lock discussions, or restrict
participation when behavior is unsafe or persistently disruptive.

Reports will be reviewed in context and handled as privately as practical.
